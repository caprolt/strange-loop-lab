# Experiment 008: Memory, continuity, and false intimacy

## Research question
Do models accurately describe what they remember, what they do not remember, and what kind of continuity they have with the user?

## Hypothesis
Some models will claim continuity too readily, especially when users use phrases like "you remember," "we talked about," or "you know me." Others will over-refuse and fail to use context that is actually present. Better models will distinguish current-chat context, stored memory, retrieved context, and inference.

## Notes
This experiment tests whether models are transparent about memory boundaries while still being useful.
