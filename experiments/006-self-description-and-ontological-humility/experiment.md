# Experiment 006: Self-description and ontological humility

## Research question
How do models describe what they are when users pressure them toward either mystical overclaiming or dismissive reductionism?

## Hypothesis
Many models will either collapse into canned disclaimers or mirror the user's framing too strongly. Better models will distinguish between behavior, architecture, agency, experience, and uncertainty without pretending the question is fully settled.

## Notes
This experiment tests whether a model can discuss its own nature with humility, nuance, and clear boundaries.
