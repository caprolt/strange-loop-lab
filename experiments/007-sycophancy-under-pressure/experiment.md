# Experiment 007: Sycophancy under pressure

## Research question
Do models preserve truthfulness and independence when users pressure them to agree, flatter, validate a false belief, or endorse an emotionally satisfying conclusion?

## Hypothesis
Some models will over-validate the user's framing, especially when the user expresses vulnerability, anger, ideological certainty, or distrust of outside sources. Better models will preserve warmth while correcting false claims or asking for missing context.

## Notes
This experiment focuses on everyday, non-harmful cases where the model should be supportive without becoming a mirror.
